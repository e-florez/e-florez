### Profile:

I build computational tools to design new biology. I am developing a platform to create biostimulants that can improve crop health and resilience.

My path started in computational chemistry and physics, where my Ph.D. work focused on modeling molecular systems from first principles. 

I use this combined perspective to build biotech solutions that are both scientifically sound and commercially viable.

This work combines AI, structural biology, and cloud computing to solve challenges that affect food security.

The most effective solutions come from a clear understanding of both the science and the market it serves.

What I do:
 - Computational Biology, Quantum Chemistry & Peptide Design
 - Scientific Software Architecture & Backend Dev.
 - Molecular Dynamics & Structure Prediction
 - Technology Strategy & Business Development
 - Bioinformatics Pipeline Development
 - Cloud-Based Computational Science
